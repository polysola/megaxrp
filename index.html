How to Buy MEGASUI on the SUI Network

Follow these steps to buy MEGASUI, the meme token of resistance, on the SUI network. This guide will take you through everything from setting up your wallet to buying MEGASUI on Sun.io.

Step 1: Set Up a SUI-Compatible Wallet

Before you can buy MEGASUI, you'll need a wallet that supports the SUI network. We recommend using Sui Wallet for the best experience.

  • Download Sui Wallet from the Sui Wallet website.
  • Install the browser extension or mobile app, and create your wallet.
  • Securely back up your recovery phrase or private key!

Step 2: Get SUI for MEGASUI

You'll need SUI to buy MEGASUI and cover transaction fees on the SUI network. You can get SUI in two ways: Buy directly or Bridge other crypto into SUI.

Option 1: Buy SUI on a Centralized Exchange

  • Sign up on a CEX like Binance or KuCoin.
  • Deposit fiat or crypto and buy SUI.
  • Withdraw SUI to your SUI wallet address (from Sui Wallet).

Option 2: Bridge Crypto to SUI Using https://portalbridge.com/

  • Visit https://portalbridge.com/ and connect your wallet.
  • Select the crypto you have (BTC, ETH, USDT) and convert it to SUI.
  • Confirm the swap and wait for SUI to arrive in your SUI wallet.

Step 3: Access MovePump

Once you've got SUI in your wallet, you're ready to swap it for MEGASUI. Head to MovePump and follow these steps:

  • Go to the MovePumppage and connect your Sui Wallet wallet.
  • Select SUI as the currency you want to swap.
  • Paste the contract address for MEGASUI into the “To” field.
  • Enter the amount of SUI you want to swap and confirm the transaction.

Step 4: Verify Your MEGASUI Balance

After the swap is complete, you’ll want to check if the MEGASUI tokens have arrived in your wallet. If you don’t see them, you may need to manually add the token using the contract address.
